
初阶：选择正确的连接词

**目标**：掌握最常用的连接词（and, but, because, so, in conclusion）

#### 练习题1：选词填空

```
从方框中选择正确的连接词填空：
[and, but, because, so, in conclusion]

1. I like reading novels, ______ my brother prefers history books. (答案：but)

2. She studies hard ______ she wants to get good grades. (答案：because)

3. He was tired, ______ he went to bed early. (答案：so)

4. I enjoy both fiction ______ non-fiction books. (答案：and)

5. ______, I believe that reading is important for everyone. (答案：In conclusion)
```

#### 练习题2：合并句子

```
用括号里的连接词把两个句子合并成一个：

1. I like coffee. I don't like tea. (but)
   → I like coffee, but I don't like tea.

2. She was hungry. She made a sandwich. (so)
   → She was hungry, so she made a sandwich.

3. He passed the exam. He studied very hard. (because)
   → He passed the exam because he studied very hard.

4. I bought a book. I read it in one day. (and)
   → I bought a book and read it in one day.
```

#### 练习题3：改错（来自学生真实错误）

```
找出句子中连接词的问题并改正：

1. I like to read, I think reading is important. (缺少连接词)
   → I like to read, and I think reading is important.

2. If novels are popular because they tell good stories. (If多余)
   → Novels are popular because they tell good stories.

3. The book was interesting, I finished it quickly. (缺少连接词)
   → The book was interesting, so I finished it quickly.
```

### 中阶：区分相似连接词

**目标**：掌握易混淆的连接词（while/whereas, because/as/since, however/nevertheless）

#### 练习题1：选词填空（易混淆词）

```
选择正确的词填空：

[while / whereas]（两者都表示对比，但whereas更正式）

1. ______ I was reading, my brother was watching TV. (答案：While)

2. Some people prefer paper books, ______ others like e-books. (答案：whereas)

[because / since / as]（都表示原因，但since和as更常用于已知信息）

3. ______ you're already here, let's start the meeting. (答案：Since/As)

4. He was late ______ he missed the bus. (答案：because)

[however / nevertheless]（都表示转折，但however更常用）

5. The book was long. ______, it was very interesting. (答案：However)

6. It was raining; ______, we went for a walk. (答案：nevertheless)
```

#### 练习题2：改写句子

```
用括号里的连接词改写句子，保持意思不变：

1. Because it was raining, we stayed inside. (so)
   → It was raining, so we stayed inside.

2. He is rich, but he is not happy. (however)
   → He is rich. However, he is not happy.

3. She studied hard, so she passed the exam. (because)
   → She passed the exam because she studied hard.
```

#### 练习题3：改错（来自学生真实错误）

```
找出并改正连接词错误：

1. ...since it's not about the things you read in childhood. (应该用like/unlike)
   → ...since it's not like the things you read in childhood.
   → ...unlike the things you read in childhood.

2. And while e-books are convenient... (and和while重复)
   → While e-books are convenient...
   → E-books are convenient, and...

3. I think online learning is better because you can choose your environment. (正确，无需修改)
```

### 高阶：段落连贯性训练

**目标**：在段落层面正确使用连接词，实现逻辑流畅

#### 练习题1：段落填空

```
在空白处填入合适的连接词（可能多个答案）：

Reading books has many benefits. ______, it improves vocabulary and critical thinking. 
______, it can reduce stress and improve empathy. ______, some people prefer watching 
movies ______ they are faster. ______, I believe that nothing can replace the experience 
of reading a good book. ______, I try to read at least 30 minutes every day.

（可能答案：First/Firstly, Second/Additionally, However, because, Therefore, So/Thus）
```

#### 练习题2：重组段落

```
把下面的句子重新排序，并用合适的连接词连接成一段连贯的文字：

A. Reading before bed helps me relax.
B. I learn new things from books.
C. I read every night.
D. Books are my favorite hobby.

→ I read every night because reading before bed helps me relax. 
  Also, I learn new things from books. 
  In conclusion, books are my favorite hobby.
```

#### 练习题3：学生段落改写（来自真实语料）

```
下面的段落来自学生S003。请用合适的连接词改写，使逻辑更清晰：

"我 think that in the future people will come back to reading long books. With long books 
you have a longer story. You can enjoy it for a long time. An article is only one part. 
It's normally factual. A novel has an exciting story. It gives you the whole story."

改写示例：
I think that in the future, people will come back to reading long books because they offer 
a longer story that you can enjoy for a long time. While an article is usually short and 
factual, a novel provides an exciting story from beginning to end.
```

#### 练习题4：自由写作引导

```
主题：Compare reading paper books and e-books.

要求：写一段话（5-7句），使用以下连接词：
- 至少1个对比连接词（while, whereas, however）
- 至少1个因果连接词（because, so, therefore）
- 至少1个递进连接词（also, moreover, furthermore）
- 1个总结连接词（in conclusion, overall）

AI引导示例：
学生：I like paper books. E-books are convenient.
AI反馈：很好！可以用"while"来对比："While e-books are convenient, I prefer paper books." 
试试用"because"解释原因，用"also"补充另一个优点。
```
