2.1 主谓一致（单数主语）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. Online learning _____ many advantages.
A. have      B. has      C. are

2. Reading books _____ my favorite hobby.
A. is      B. are      C. be

3. The teacher _____ us every day.
A. help      B. helps      C. helping

答案：1-B, 2-A, 3-B
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. My brother _____ (play) basketball every weekend.
2. The information on the internet _____ (be) very useful.
3. Each of the students _____ (have) a different opinion.

答案：1-plays, 2-is, 3-has
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：描述你每天的学习习惯。
提示词：my favorite subject, online learning, the teacher
要求：用这三个短语造三个句子，注意主谓一致。

AI引导示例：
学生：My favorite subject are math.
AI反馈：很好！但"my favorite subject"是单数，应该用"is"。试试看："My favorite subject is math."
```

### 2.2 使役动词（make/let）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. My parents _____ me study every day.
A. make      B. make to      C. makes

2. The teacher let us _____ in groups.
A. work      B. to work      C. working

3. This course _____ me improve my skills.
A. made      B. made to      C. make

答案：1-A, 2-A, 3-A
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. My coach _____ (让) me practice every day. (用make)
2. Please _____ (让) me know your decision. (用let)
3. The movie _____ (使得) me cry.

答案：1-makes, 2-let, 3-made
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：描述别人对你的影响。
提示词：make me, let me
要求：用make me和let me各造一个句子，描述老师或父母对你的帮助。

AI引导示例：
学生：My teacher make me to do homework.
AI反馈：很好！但有两个小问题：
1. "my teacher"是单数，动词应该用"makes"
2. "make"后面不需要"to"
正确应该是："My teacher makes me do homework."
现在请用"let me"再试一个句子。
```

### 2.3 易混淆词（affected/effective, prove/use）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. This method is very _____.
A. affected      B. effective      C. effect

2. We should _____ technology in the classroom.
A. prove      B. use      C. proof

3. His speech deeply _____ the audience.
A. effected      B. affected      C. effective

答案：1-B, 2-B, 3-B
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. Self-directed learning can be very _____ (有效的) if you are disciplined.
2. We need to _____ (使用) what we learn in real life.
3. The new policy will _____ (影响) all students.

答案：1-effective, 2-use/apply, 3-affect
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：讨论学习方法的效果。
提示词：effective, use, affect
要求：用这三个词各造一个句子，描述你的学习经验。

AI引导示例：
学生：I use computer to study. It is effective.
AI反馈：很好的尝试！可以再把句子丰富一下：
"Using a computer to study is very effective because..."
试着用"affect"造一个关于老师如何影响你的句子。
```

### 2.4 介词错误（focus on, at the same time）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. You should focus _____ your studies.
A. at      B. on      C. in

2. I can listen to music and study _____ the same time.
A. in      B. on      C. at

3. We need a break _____ screens.
A. of      B. from      C. on

答案：1-B, 2-C, 3-B
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. Please focus _____ (在) your homework.
2. I eat breakfast and read news _____ _____ _____ _____ (同时).
3. Reading paper books gives me a break _____ (远离) screens.

答案：1-on, 2-at the same time, 3-from
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：描述你的日常习惯。
提示词：focus on, at the same time, a break from
要求：用这三个短语描述你的一天。

AI引导示例：
学生：I focus on study and listen music at the same time.
AI反馈：很好！注意"listen"后面要加"to"哦。"I focus on studying and listen to music at the same time." 
现在试试用"a break from"造一个句子。
```

### 2.5 词性混淆（good/well）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. She speaks English very _____.
A. good      B. well      C. goodly

2. This is a _____ book.
A. good      B. well      C. goodly

3. He did _____ on the test.
A. good      B. well      C. goodly

答案：1-B, 2-A, 3-B
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. She is a _____ (好的) teacher.
2. She teaches _____ (好).
3. I don't feel very _____ (好) today.

答案：1-good, 2-well, 3-well (feel是系动词，后面用形容词good也可，但well更常见)
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：描述你的老师或朋友。
提示词：good, well
要求：用good造一个句子描述人，用well造一个句子描述动作。

AI引导示例：
学生：My teacher is good. She teach well.
AI反馈：很好！注意"she"是第三人称单数，动词应该是"teaches"哦。
"My teacher is good. She teaches well." 完美！
```

### 2.6 搭配错误（stay with, on the right path）

**级别1：选择题**
```
1. These memories will _____ with me forever.
A. keep      B. stay      C. remain

2. The teacher put me on the right _____.
A. road      B. path      C. way

3. Too much screen time will _____ me blind.
A. get      B. make      C. let

答案：1-B, 2-B, 3-B
```

**级别2：填空题**
```
1. Good habits will _____ (伴随) you for life.
2. A mentor can put you on the right _____ (道路).
3. Reading in dim light might _____ (使) your eyes tired.

答案：1-stay with, 2-path, 3-make
```

**级别3：引导造句**
```
场景：谈论对你影响深远的人或事。
提示词：stay with me, put me on the right path
要求：用这两个短语造两个句子。

AI引导示例：
学生：My teacher put me on the right road.
AI反馈：很好！但英语中更常说"on the right path"。试试看：
"My teacher put me on the right path."
现在用"stay with me"造一个句子。
```
